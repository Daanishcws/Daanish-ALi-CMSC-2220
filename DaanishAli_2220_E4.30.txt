ORG 100

If, Load X
    Subt Y
    Skipcond 800
    Jump Else1

    Load Y
    Add One
    Store Y
    Clear

Else1, Load X
    Subt Z
    Skipcond 400
    Jump Else2

    Load Y
    Subt One
    Store Y
    Clear

Else2, Load Z
    Add One
    Store Z
    Halt

X, Dec 0
Y, Dec 0
Z, Dec 0
One, Dec 1
